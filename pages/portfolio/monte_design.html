Monte Desgin

Monte Design Group was founded in 2005 by designers Ralph and Michelle Montemurro. Originating from the need to furnish their son's modern nursery, they launched a line of Canadian-made high-quality furniture. Their belief is that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, and their goal is to produce exceptionally comfortable and design-driven products that you will be proud to own.

At Monte Design Group, they are passionate about beautiful design, superior quality and excellent customer service.
